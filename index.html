Data Explanation

  • Temperature: Room temperature in degrees Celsius.
  • Humidity: Air humidity in percentage.
  • Gas (RAW): Raw gas value from the MQ-135 sensor.
  • Gas (Compensated): Gas value compensated based on temperature and humidity.
  • Air Quality: Air quality status based on compensated gas value.
